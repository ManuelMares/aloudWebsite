What is AAC?

A digital Augmentative/Alternative Communication (AAC) system is a special software that allows people with special needs to communicate through the use of images, word-buttons, and by using digital voices

About Us

Aloud began with the belief that every person deserves a voice. Inspired by real communication challenges, our diverse team of engineers, designers, and researchers came together to build tools that restore independence and dignity. United by empathy and expertise in AI and accessibility, we transform innovative ideas into solutions that bridge the communication gap for people with muscle impairments.

At Aloud, our mission is to enhance communication for non-verbal individuals, with a special focus on people with muscle impairments. We are committed to using innovative technology to empower voices that are often unheard.
